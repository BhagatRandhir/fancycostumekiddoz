Rental Policy

Please read our rental terms carefully to ensure a smooth and happy experience for your little one.

📦 Booking & Availability

  • Costumes can be booked via WhatsApp by clicking the Book on WhatsApp button on any costume card.
  • All bookings are confirmed only after a reply from our team confirming availability.
  • We recommend booking at least 2–3 days in advance to ensure your preferred costume is available.
  • Availability is shown on each costume card (✅ Available / ❌ Unavailable).

💰 Pricing & Payment

  • Rental price is listed as ₹ per day on each costume card.
  • A security deposit may be collected depending on the costume value — refunded on return in good condition.
  • Payment can be made via UPI, bank transfer, or cash on delivery (as agreed with our team).
  • Multi-day discounts are available — ask us on WhatsApp for special rates.

🚚 Delivery & Return

  • We deliver across India. Delivery charges apply based on your location.
  • Costumes must be returned within the agreed rental period.
  • Late returns will be charged at the daily rental rate for each extra day.
  • Costumes are delivered in a sealed bag — please check and confirm condition on receipt.
  • Return the costume in the same packaging it was delivered in.

👗 Care & Condition

  • Costumes are professionally cleaned and sanitised before every rental.
  • Please do not wash, bleach, or iron the costumes.
  • Minor wear and tear during normal use is acceptable.
  • Any stains, tears, or damage beyond normal use will be charged at the repair or replacement cost.
  • If a costume is lost or irreparably damaged, the full replacement cost will be charged.

❌ Cancellation Policy

  • Cancellations made 48+ hours before the rental start date — full refund.
  • Cancellations made 24–48 hours before — 50% refund.
  • Cancellations made less than 24 hours before — no refund.
  • In case of unavailability on our end, a full refund will be issued immediately.

👶 Safety & Suitability

  • All costumes are made from child-safe, non-toxic materials.
  • Please refer to our Size Guide before booking to ensure proper fit.
  • Adult supervision is recommended while children wear costumes.
  • We are not responsible for any injuries caused by improper use of costumes.

Kids Size Guide

📐 How to Measure Your Child

  • Chest: Measure around the fullest part of the chest, keeping the tape level.
  • Waist: Measure around the natural waistline (narrowest part of the torso).
  • Height: Measure from head to toe standing straight.
  • Tip: If your child is between two sizes, choose the larger size for comfort.

💡 Size Tips

  • Fancy dress costumes are designed with slightly relaxed fit for easy wearing over clothes if needed.
  • For princess gowns and robes, length is important — always check height.
  • For superhero and character suits, chest measurement is the most important factor.
  • Kids grow fast — if your child is at the top of a size range, go up one size.
  • Not sure? Send us your child's age, height and weight on WhatsApp — we'll recommend the perfect size!

Born from Bedtime Stories & Big Dreams

It all started when our founders, parents of young children, struggled to find quality costumes for school events and birthday parties that wouldn't break the bank — especially for a one-time occasion.

The solution? A rental service! Kids get to wear stunning, professionally-made costumes without parents spending a fortune on something worn once.

Today, Fancy Costume for Kiddoz delivers rental costumes across India — from school plays and birthday bashes to themed photoshoots and cultural events.
